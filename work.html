My Work

Slang

Slang was an app created in my Intro to UX Design course to help teachers and parents understand the slang their children and students speak. Through user and competitor research, persona creation, usability testing, principles of information architecture, wireframes and prototypes were created and presented to stakeholders.

Mind Body Soul

In an effort to meet the mental and physical health needs of women, in addition to giving them the ability to document their medical data all in one place, Mind Body Soul was created as a holistic, transformative wellness app. The following skills were learned during the iterative design process: user-centered design, emotional and visual design, competitive analysis, generative and evaluative research, user personas and business requirements, mobile-first and responsive design, information architecture and user flows, iterative wireframing and prototyping, usability and preference testing, and design documentation and presentation.
